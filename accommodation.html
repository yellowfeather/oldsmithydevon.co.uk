The accommodation comprises of:

  • Kitchen Diner

    A modern high gloss kitchen equipped with a microwave, integral electric oven / hob / dishwasher /fridge / washing machine / pull out larder / limestone flooring and oak work tops.

  • Sitting Room

    This is a large double aspect room with glass doors onto the garden. There is a wall mounted flat screen TV, Family SKY package, DVD & Wi-Fi connection. There are two large and very comfortable sofas. The stone fireplace, slate floor and vaulted ceiling add drama and character to this spectacular space.

  • Bedroom 1

    Is on the ground floor and accessed off the sitting room. It has a KING SIZE bed, large built in wardrobe and modern EN SUITE shower room. This room is ideally situated for any guest with impaired mobility.

  • Bedroom 2

    This is a double aspect TWIN ROOM with vaulted ceiling. There are black out blinds at both windows. This room is accessed via the mezzanine landing on the first floor.

  • The Mezzanine

    The Mezzanine overlooks the sitting room and is an ideal space for a children's play area or for quiet relaxation. There is seating and shelving filled with books for you to read at your leisure.

  • Bathroom

    This is a large family bathroom with contemporary fittings including a free standing bath and limestone flooring.

  • Garden

    Outside is a private walled courtyard garden. There is a large seating area and BBQ. There is a store box with beach toys, crabbing nets and garden cushions. There is also a bicycle wash down area. The entrance to the Cottage leads into a secure covered porch which is large enough and ideal for drying Wet Suits and Rain coats and for storing Surf Boards, Boots and beach accessories.

  • Parking

    There is a private drive for one large car and there is an open fronted garage with bike lock facilities. It is large enough to store Canoes, Surf Boards, Motorbikes or a Dinghy.

The cottage benefits from being fully central heated.

Bedding, duvet covers and towels are included.
